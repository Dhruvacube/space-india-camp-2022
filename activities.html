Activities

Here are some amazing activities there at the camp

MOON WALK CHAIR

We bring to you once in lifetime experience of walking on the moon. A 1/6 gravity chair shows Space Camp attendees what it might be like to walk across the moon by using springs to simulate the reduced gravity.

SPACE WALK

Why don’t we become the ISS astronauts that we always dreamed of…. Here the participants will climb on space station model wearing a space suit and perform maintenance simulation on it.

VISIT TO SCIENCE CITY

We the organizers will take the participants on a tour of the Science city and indulge the growth of imaginative and scientific curiosity of the young minds. Dome Theatre, Laser Theatre,3D Theatre, Climate Change Theatre, Earthquake Simulator, Flight Simulator, Robotic Dinosaurs to name a few of the exciting adventures we will embark upon.

Astronaut Multi-Axis Trainer

Zero-gravity or “the feeling of weightlessness” is surely a lifetime experience but to navigate there needs to be learnt and to teach all the participants this maneuver we bring this event. Here the Participants will have opportunity to take a ride on Multi Axis Trainer.
